ABOUT INVESTPOOL

INVESTPOOL Ghana is a company incorporated in Ghana in November 1995 to close the gap between the rich and the poor. It is mandated by the laws of Ghana to float financial and economic deals among parties on a singular platform.
For example, someone having 10 cedis in his pocket may choose to open our platform to see the day's DEALSavailable that can make use of the money in his pocket.
The company serves as a guarantee for the two parties following vigorous analysis of their businesses and economic value.Through these interventions, geographical challenges are totally eliminated, as citizen can trade and do business by click of a button from any part of the country under certified conditions.

Regional Destinations however are activated to offer deals close to every investor.
